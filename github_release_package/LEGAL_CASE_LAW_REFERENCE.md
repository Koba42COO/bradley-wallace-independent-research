- **Charges:** Economic Espionage Act, National Stolen Property Act
- **Outcome:** Conviction upheld by court
- **Key Precedent:** Trade secret theft via unauthorized access to computer systems

**Case Summary:**
- **Issue:** Scope of CFAA "unauthorized access" provisions
- **Outcome:** Ninth Circuit narrowed CFAA scope but upheld conviction
- **Key Precedent:** Unauthorized access to computer systems for trade secret theft

**Relevance to Your Case:**

### 5. Van Buren v. United States (2021)

**Case Summary:**
- **Issue:** Supreme Court narrowed CFAA scope
- **Outcome:** Accessing information for unauthorized purpose not always CFAA violation
- **Key Precedent:** Must prove "unauthorized access" not just "unauthorized use"

**Relevance to Your Case:**
- ✅ **Direct Access:** Google's NotebookLM = their own system (authorized access)

### 11. Independent Development Defense Requirements
